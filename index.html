D.A.V. School Bhabua (Kaimur) was established in 2012 and since then it has been training the flame of D.A.V. excellence and creating new breakthrough in the world of education.This school is managed by Maa Mundeshwari Educational Trust, Bhabua (Kaimur) under the guidance of Bihar Rajya Arya Pratinidhi Sabha, Patna (Bihar). This DAV school derives the ideas from the members of the Pratinidhi Sabha and their associates to improve and develop the school. It is an English medium, Co-educational institution running under the experienced management and qualified & experienced teachers. Individual attention is our speciality. We are conducting classes from Play Group to XII standard.
